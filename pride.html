Welcome to Honolulu's LGBTQ+ pride parade.

We've partnered with the Hawaii LGBT Legacy Foundation to bring you the best pride parade Hawai'i has ever had. With performances from local community groups, sponsors from local businesses, and the greatest LGBTQ+ speakers, Shangri-La Pride's mission is to make Honolulu a place for all queer individuals to feel the aloha spirit.
